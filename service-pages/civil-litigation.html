Legal Framework in Pakistan

Civil litigation in Pakistan is governed by various laws and procedural rules:

  • Code of Civil Procedure, 1908: Governs the procedure for civil litigation in Pakistan
  • Contract Act, 1872: Governs the formation and enforcement of contracts
  • Specific Relief Act, 1877: Provides remedies for specific performance and injunctions
  • Transfer of Property Act, 1882: Governs the transfer of property rights
  • Limitation Act, 1908: Sets time limits for filing civil suits
  • Court Rules: Each court has its own rules of procedure

Our team has in-depth knowledge of these laws and procedural rules, ensuring that your case is handled efficiently and effectively in the Pakistani legal system.
