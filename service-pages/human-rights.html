Fundamental Rights

We provide legal protection and enforcement of fundamental rights guaranteed under the Constitution of Pakistan:

  • Right to Life: Protection of the right to life and personal liberty (Article 9)
  • Freedom of Speech: Protection of freedom of speech and expression (Article 19)
  • Freedom of Association: Protection of freedom of association (Article 17)
  • Freedom of Religion: Protection of freedom of religion and belief (Article 20)
  • Right to Fair Trial: Protection of the right to fair trial and due process (Article 10A)
  • Right to Privacy: Protection of the right to privacy (Article 14)
  • Writ Petitions: Filing of writ petitions in High Courts and Supreme Court for enforcement of fundamental rights

Our team has extensive experience in constitutional litigation, ensuring that your fundamental rights are protected and enforced through the judicial system.

Gender Rights

We provide legal protection and advocacy for gender rights under Pakistani law:

  • Gender Discrimination: Legal action against gender-based discrimination
  • Workplace Harassment: Legal protection against workplace harassment under the Protection against Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act, 2010
  • Domestic Violence: Legal protection against domestic violence under the Domestic Violence (Prevention and Protection) Act, 2012
  • Women's Inheritance: Protection of women's inheritance rights under Islamic law and Pakistani statutes
  • Child Marriage: Legal action against child marriages under the Child Marriage Restraint Act, 1929
  • Gender-Based Violence: Legal protection against gender-based violence and abuse
  • Transgender Rights: Protection of transgender rights under the Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Act, 2018

Our team is committed to protecting and advancing gender rights in Pakistan, ensuring equality and justice for all genders.

Legal Framework in Pakistan

Human rights in Pakistan are protected by various constitutional provisions, laws, and international treaties:

  • Constitution of Pakistan, 1973: Fundamental rights guaranteed under Articles 8-28
  • Protection against Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act, 2010: Protection against workplace harassment
  • Domestic Violence (Prevention and Protection) Act, 2012: Protection against domestic violence
  • Juvenile Justice System Ordinance, 2000: Protection of juvenile offenders' rights
  • Transgender Persons (Protection of Rights) Act, 2018: Protection of transgender rights
  • Child Marriage Restraint Act, 1929: Protection against child marriages
  • International Treaties: Pakistan's obligations under international human rights treaties
  • Islamic Law: Islamic principles of human dignity and justice

Our team has in-depth knowledge of these laws and constitutional provisions, ensuring that your human rights are protected and enforced under Pakistani law.
