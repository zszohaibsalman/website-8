Legal Framework in Pakistan

Corporate law in Pakistan is governed by various laws and regulations:

  • Companies Act, 2017: The primary legislation governing companies in Pakistan
  • Securities Act, 2015: Governs securities regulation and capital markets
  • Competition Act, 2010: Governs competition and anti-trust matters
  • Contract Act, 1872: Governs the formation and enforcement of contracts
  • Income Tax Ordinance, 2001: Governs income tax matters
  • Sales Tax Act, 1990: Governs sales tax matters
  • Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1947: Governs foreign exchange matters
  • Intellectual Property Laws: Various laws governing intellectual property rights

Our team has in-depth knowledge of these laws and regulations, ensuring that your business operates in compliance with Pakistani law.
